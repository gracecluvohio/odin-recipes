Tuxedosam Macaron Ice Cream Sandwiches

Description

A cooling yet refined treat for the summer, perfect for a dapper penguin like Tuxedosam!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 0.5 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 5 egg whites
  • A tub of vanilla ice cream
  • Blue food dye

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 365 degrees F.
  2. Mix all the ingredients (besides the ice cream) together.
  3. Pipe the mixture in little 1 inch circles.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Let the shells cool 15 minutes.
  6. Put a dollop of ice cream on each shell and top with another shell.
